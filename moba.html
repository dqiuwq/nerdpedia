MOBA Games


Multiplayer Online Battle Arena or MOBA in short, is also known as Action Real-time Strategy (ARTS) for short, is a sub-genre of the real-time strategy(RTS) genre. It differs from RTS games in that there are no unit construction(s) and players control just one character. It is like a fusion of both action and real-time strategy games. This genre emphasizes more on cooperative team-play with the main objective being to destory the opponents' main structure with assistance of periodically spawned computer-controlled units.

Gameplay Of Moba Games

There are two opposing teams whose goal as a team is to destroy the enemy base to win. Though some games have the option of different victory conditions, typiccaly, there is one main structured that must be destroyed to win. Destroying of other structures within the enemy base may confer other benefits but defensive structures are in place to prevent this, as well as the inclusion of reletively weak computer-controlled units which spawn periodically at each base and travel down predefined paths.


Players control a single power in-game unit called a 'hero'. When a hero stands near a killed enemy unit or kills an enemy unit, it gains experience points to level up, upon leveling up, they have the ability to learn more powerful skills/abilities. When a hero dies, they wait a designated time which increases as they level up/


They also earn money per second, or by killing units or enemy 'hero' units. The gold earned is used to purchase items, that differs in price based on impact it has on the game.
