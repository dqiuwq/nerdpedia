Dragon Nest SEA

Dragon Nest is a free to play fantasy MMORPG developed by EyeDentity.
Dragon Nest incorporates non-targeting system gameplay to create a fast paced
action filled experience. Players choose from a range of hero classes, characters
that can equip customizable gear and weapons. Devastating skills can also be learned,
to increase one's power when defeating monsters in dungeons or defeating other players in PVP.

 

 

Dragon Nest classes are divided into 4 classes :

Warrior which uses will battle foes in fast-paced close-ranged melee combat.

Archers which excel at long-ranged combat, taking out foes using a bow and arrows and using your speed.
